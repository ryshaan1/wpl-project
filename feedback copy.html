Frequently Asked Questions
How do I cancel a booking?+
Cancel via My Bookings up to 30 minutes before your slot — no charge. After that, a 50% fee applies. Refunds take 3–5 business days to process.
When will Malabar Hill be back online?+
VG-01 Malabar Hill is currently under scheduled maintenance. We expect it back online within 48 hours. Use the Get Notified option on the station page to be alerted.
Which connectors are supported at each station?+
Malabar Hill: Type 2 AC (11 kW). Vidyavihar: CCS2 DC (60 kW). Chembur: CCS2 DC Fast (150 kW). All support standard Indian EV models including Tata Nexon EV, MG ZS EV, and Ather.
How is pricing calculated?+
You pay per kWh consumed, plus a flat ₹5 platform fee and 18% GST. Rates are ₹10/kWh (Malabar Hill), ₹15/kWh (Vidyavihar), and ₹18/kWh (Chembur). The booking page shows an estimate upfront.
Can I extend my charging session?+
Extensions are available if the next slot is unbooked. Request at the station kiosk. Extensions are billed at the normal per-kWh rate. Please be considerate of other users waiting.
